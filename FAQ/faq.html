Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How can I reserve a parking spot?

A: To reserve a parking spot, simply navigate to our "Book Parking" page and follow the instructions to select your desired location, date, and time.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and mobile payment options. You can choose your preferred method during the checkout process.

Q: Is there a cancellation policy?

A: Yes, we have a cancellation policy in place. You can cancel your parking reservation for a full refund up to 24 hours before your scheduled arrival time.

Q: Can I receive a receipt for my parking reservation?

A: Yes, you will receive a confirmation email upon completing your booking, which serves as your receipt. You will also recieve a Confirmation Number on your Phone.

Q: Can I cancel my parking reservation if my plans change?

A: Yes, you can cancel your parking reservation according to the cancellation policy outlined during the booking process. Refund eligibility and any applicable cancellation fees will be clearly stated in the terms and conditions.
