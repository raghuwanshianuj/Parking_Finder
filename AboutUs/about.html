Our Objectives:

    Real-Time Parking Availability

  • Utilize sensors, cameras, or user-reported data to provide real-time information on parking slot availability.
  • Integrate with parking management systems or smart city infrastructure for accurate data.

    • Slot Reservation System

  • Enable users to search for available parking slots based on location, time, and preferences.
  • Implement a reservation system allowing users to book and pay for parking slots in advance.

    • Navigation and Directions

  • Integrate with navigation services (Google Maps, Waze, etc.) to provide users with turn-by-turn directions to their reserved parking spot.

    • Payment Integration

  • Integrate secure payment gateways to facilitate transactions for parking reservations.

    • Notification System:

  • Implement a notification system to alert users about successful reservations.
